About this event

"Harnessing the Future through Cloud Computing."

The Quantum Information Technology Convention is a 3-day and 2-night activity. Discussions from keynote speakers of well-known IT companies worldwide fill the convention with essential ideas for the people interested in this field. The convention aims to challenge the delegates’ competence, present them with additional knowledge, and sharpen their skills in the IT field.

Our speakers

Alexander Mark de Leon

DISPLAY SOLUTIONS, GOOGLE PHILIPPINES

Alex is a recent balikbayan who just moved back to the Philippines after almost eight years in the states. Alex started his career at Deloitte & Touche in Boston, doing technology consulting for their public sector practice. He has been at Google for four years, and was mostly recently working on YouTube partnerships in Mountain View, California. After transferring to Manila last month, Alex currently manages Google's display advertising business.

Herald Uy

CEO, KOLLAB

Herald Uy is a consultant for Google Enterprise and Google for Education, and a distinguished evangelist-at-large for the Google Business Groups communities in the Philippines. Formerly the chief technology evangelist of KFC + Mister Donut + Tokyo Tokyo, Herald ushered in IT consumerization, mobility and cloud-based collaboration for the F&B giant. His stint as principal architect of Singularity Consulting earned accolades in the Google community as one of the most successful enterprise partners in the APAC region. Herald is concurrently the CEO of Kollab, a Google for Education partner, and the cloud transformation program director of Globe Telecom. He also maintains a tech blog over at singler.ph.

John Paul Sibug

AUDIENCE MARKETING MANAGER, MICROSOFT

JP is the Audience Marketing Manager under Microsoft Philippines, handling various groups of people like students, faculty members, IT Professionals, developers, and start-up businesses in the entire country. His business acumen and diverse network connections helped boost these groups in both cost efficiency and people development segments. His leadership still upholds its purpose as he is still very much into varied endeavors for fellow Filipinos.

Johnn Mendoza

CEO, REMINISENSE CORPORATION

Johnn is a bookworm, widow musician, animal-lover, photoblogger, food blogger, wildlife blogger, biologist, freediver, community catalyst, start-up guy, Digital marketing crazy kid, and a proud apostle of God. He pursued almost a year in a UK Digital Marketing company based in Cebu and finally went into Digital Marketing consultancy while starting a Start-up company: Reminisense Corporation, a cloud-development company that created the first ever online queuing software for public consumption: www.FeatherQ.com Johnn now spends most of his time working on his Apostolate through his learnings in Sugbu Study Center, freediving in as many bodies of waters in the Philippines, and discovering more of the world as possible.
